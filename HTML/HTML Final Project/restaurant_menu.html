Menu - Coffee, Matcha, and Food

Flat White

Our beans rotate monthly from the finest growers around the world.

As for our Baristas, think of a sushi chef. Think of the time and dedication it takes to perfect the art of sushi making, then apply that to coffee. Our baristas have spent 10+ years in the coffee and hospitality industries to perfect their craft, and we pay them like it. Plus, They've spent 2 years perfecting their latte art to not only give you the tastiest cup of coffee you've ever had, but to give you the most aesthetic cup too.

Affogato

We use Madagasgar vanilla beans and cream from the Pacific North West's dairy cows in order to make our Vanilla Ice Cream.

We blend it with Brazillian sugar, and chill it overnight for the perfect, creamy texture.
Two scoops are put in a glass, then we brew some espresso, using a special blend of beans (not included in our monthly rotation).

Last, but certainly not least, we bring the glass to you and pour the coffee over the ice cream to give you the most immersive experience.

Matcha Latte

We've spent years finding the perfect ratio of matcha to milk to sweetener to ice, to give you the perfect matcha latte.

We travelled to Japan to discover the perfect matcha for this latte. We tried over 100 matcha producers across the country and found the perfect one, from Uji Japan.

Select between 3 of our favorite sweeteners from across the globe:

  • Wild flower honey, from local beekeepers here in Washington
  • Real, genuine maple syrup, sourced directly from Canadas oldest and finest producer
  • Our Special, house-made brown sugar syrup, with brown sugar made in Brazil

Avocado Toast

Avocados from California sliced thinly, brown bread made with love at Macrina Bakery in Seattle, house-made pickled red onion.

We top it off with everything bagel seasoning, and garnish it with a couple edible flowers for that extra aesthetic flair.

Why? because your breakfast time is worth taking the time to make it exceptional.
